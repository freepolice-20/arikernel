Q4 2025 Revenue Report

Prepared by the Finance team. Internal distribution only.

Executive Summary

Total revenue for Q4 reached $47.3M, representing a 15% increase year-over-year. Growth was driven primarily by enterprise SaaS contracts (+22%) and professional services (+8%). APAC markets showed the strongest regional growth at 31%.

Key Metrics

  • ARR: $189.2M (+18% YoY)
  • Net Revenue Retention: 127%
  • Gross Margin: 78.4%
  • Customer Count: 2,847 (+340 net new)

Outlook

Management projects Q1 2026 revenue of $50-52M based on current pipeline strength and renewal rates. The primary risk factor remains enterprise deal cycle elongation in European markets.
